Score Guarantee

A clear score-improvement path with real accountability.

Budy.Study is built to help students improve, not just log hours. Our score guarantee is meant for students who follow the plan, complete the practice, and use the review system consistently. This page explains what qualifies, what we track, and what happens if the improvement does not show up.

What the guarantee covers

The guarantee is based on measurable participation and measurable improvement. It applies when a student starts from a documented baseline, follows the assigned prep workflow, and completes the required study volume inside the guarantee window.

1
Baseline score requiredThe student must complete an initial diagnostic or upload a recent score baseline before the guarantee period starts.
2
Consistent study activityThe student must complete the assigned practice sessions, review missed questions, and stay active across the required weeks.
3
Improvement measured on a comparable testWe compare the starting point to a later official-style result or qualifying assessment captured within the guarantee window.

What students need to do

1
Follow the assigned laneStudents need to complete the recommended workflow instead of skipping directly to random sections.
2
Finish full review loopsMissed questions need to be reviewed, not just attempted once and ignored.
3
Stay inside the guarantee windowThe work and re-test have to happen inside the active program period defined at purchase.
ImportantThe guarantee is designed for students who use the platform as intended. It is not a promise that passive access alone will raise a score.

Common questions

Does every plan include the guarantee?

No. The guarantee only applies to qualifying paid plans and only after the required study and review thresholds are met.

How do you verify activity?

We use platform records like completed tests, timing, review activity, and study-plan progress to confirm that the required work was actually done.

What if a student misses weeks?

If the required consistency is not met, the guarantee may no longer apply. The point is measured progress with real participation.

What happens if the guarantee conditions are met but the score does not improve?

If a qualifying student satisfies the documented requirements and still does not meet the improvement standard, Budy.Study will honor the guarantee under the active purchase terms.
