Privacy Policy

How Budy.Study handles account, study, billing, and support data.

This policy explains what information Budy.Study collects, why it is used, where it may be stored, and what choices users have when they use practice tests, study tools, subscriptions, analytics, or AI-assisted learning features.

support@budy.study Terms and subscriptions
Last updated: March 31, 2026
What we collect Account, subscription, and study activity data

That includes sign-in details, test attempts, saved questions, progress data, and support requests needed to run the product.

Why we collect it To deliver the service and improve it

We use data to keep users signed in, save progress, power paid features, fix bugs, and understand which parts of the product are being used.

How to contact us Questions, deletion, or correction requests

Email support@budy.study if you need help with your data, billing records, or account removal requests.

Scope of this policy

This policy applies to the Budy.Study website, student dashboard, study hub, practice-test flows, subscription checkout, billing support, and related support or analytics tooling. It is written to cover both the current web product and future mobile app builds that may use the same Budy account system.

Account and sign-in data

We collect information required to create and maintain an account, such as email address, authentication identifiers, profile name, grade selection, and subscription state.

  • Authentication may be handled through Auth0, including email/password or Google sign-in.
  • We may store session-related data in browser storage so users stay signed in between visits.
  • We may associate account identifiers with billing, saved questions, AI usage, and test history.

Study and progress data

We store learning activity needed to make the product useful, including practice-test attempts, score history, review-test activity, flagged questions, saved questions, and other progress signals.

  • Recent attempts may be shown back to the user inside the dashboard or Study Hub.
  • Saved question and score data may be stored in hosted databases or in local browser storage depending on the feature.
  • We may use study data to recommend follow-up topics or review sessions.

Purchases and billing

When users start or manage a paid plan, we collect the minimum information needed to process subscriptions and verify entitlement status.

  • Web purchases currently route through Stripe checkout and Stripe's billing portal.
  • We may store plan level, renewal status, cancellation timing, and payment-platform metadata on the account.
  • If Budy.Study is offered through a future App Store or Google Play app, subscription handling may follow that store's purchase and refund rules instead of Stripe's web flow.

AI Deep Dive and support tools

If users trigger AI-assisted explanations, the selected word, phrase, or study context may be processed to generate a response. Usage counters may also be stored to enforce plan limits.

  • Selected learning content can include the chosen term, the surrounding question or topic, and related category labels.
  • Usage records may include the account, billing period key, and consumption counts.
  • Preview or fallback responses may be shown when a live model is not configured.

Analytics, diagnostics, and observability

We use first-party analytics and error monitoring to understand reliability issues, broken user journeys, and general feature engagement.

  • This may include page views, interaction events, device or browser metadata, and error traces.
  • We use this information to debug failures, measure adoption, and improve product quality.
  • We do not sell personal information to data brokers.

Marketing, launch, and school outreach

If users, families, or schools contact Budy.Study about launch updates, pilots, partnerships, or product interest, we may keep the contact details and request context needed to respond and measure outreach effectiveness.

  • This can include email address, school or organization name, campaign source, and the nature of the request.
  • We may use campaign and referral data to understand which acquisition efforts are actually producing qualified interest.
  • Users can request removal from non-essential outreach communications by contacting support@budy.study.

Browser storage and local device data

Some Budy.Study features use local browser storage to keep users signed in, remember settings, cache progress, or preserve in-progress study work between page loads.

  • Examples can include login/session markers, grade preference, saved-question fallbacks, or UI tutorial completion state.
  • Local data can remain on the device until it is cleared by the browser or by the user.
  • Clearing browser storage may remove convenience features or local copies of progress data.

How we use information

  • To authenticate users and protect account access.
  • To save practice history, study preferences, review queues, and progress summaries.
  • To provide subscriptions, paid entitlements, billing support, and account recovery.
  • To improve product design, reliability, and educational effectiveness.
  • To investigate fraud, abuse, policy violations, or service misuse.

Service providers and sharing

  • Authentication providers that help users sign in securely.
  • Payment processors and billing tools that support subscriptions.
  • Hosting, database, analytics, logging, and monitoring providers used to operate Budy.Study.
  • Professional advisors or authorities when needed to comply with law, enforce rights, or protect users and the service.

Retention

We keep data for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain security, preserve necessary business or billing records, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations. Some browser-stored data remains until removed locally. Aggregated or de-identified analytics may be kept longer for operational reporting.

Your choices

  • You can contact support@budy.study to request account updates, data correction, or deletion review.
  • You can sign out and clear local browser storage through your browser settings.
  • You can cancel eligible subscriptions through the available billing management flow.
  • Some information may need to be retained for security, fraud prevention, or financial recordkeeping.

Students and younger users

Budy.Study is intended for students and families. If you believe a child has provided personal information in a way that needs review or removal, contact support@budy.study so the request can be evaluated and handled appropriately.

Policy updates

We may update this policy as Budy.Study changes, including when billing flows, mobile apps, AI features, or legal requirements change. Material updates will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
