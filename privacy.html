Privacy Policy

How Budy.Study collects, uses, and protects user data.

This policy explains what we collect, why it is needed, how it is used to operate the service, and what choices users have.

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Scope

This policy applies to the Budy.Study website, practice tests, Study Hub, account dashboard, subscription flows, and support channels.

What we collect

  • Account and sign-in data (email, authentication identifiers, profile details).
  • Study data (test attempts, saved questions, flagged items, progress history).
  • Subscription and billing status data required to manage access.
  • Support communication details when users contact us.
  • Operational analytics and diagnostics needed for reliability and product improvement.

How we use information

  • To authenticate accounts and keep the service secure.
  • To save progress and personalize study recommendations.
  • To provide subscriptions and billing support.
  • To troubleshoot issues and improve product performance.
  • To investigate misuse, fraud, or policy violations.

Service providers and sharing

Budy.Study may use third-party providers for authentication, payments, hosting, storage, and analytics. We do not sell personal information to data brokers.

Retention

Data is retained for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain security, support billing records, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations.

User choices

  • Request account updates, correction, or deletion review by emailing support@budy.study.
  • Sign out and clear browser storage through browser settings.
  • Manage or cancel eligible subscriptions through the billing flow.

Students and younger users

If a parent or guardian believes data from a younger user should be reviewed or removed, contact support and include relevant account details.

Policy updates

We may update this policy as product features, legal requirements, or billing flows evolve. Changes are reflected by updating the effective date on this page.
