Privacy Policy

How Budy.Study handles student and account information.

This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used to deliver account access, study features, score tracking, and subscription support.

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Information we collect

We may collect account details such as email address, authentication identifiers, subscription status, test attempt history, and study-related profile selections used to personalize the product.

How we use information

  • To authenticate users and protect account access
  • To save test attempts, score history, and study preferences
  • To manage subscriptions, billing, and support requests
  • To improve product performance and feature quality

How information is stored

Some data may be stored locally in the browser for convenience. Account, subscription, and attempt-history data may also be stored in hosted infrastructure used by Budy.Study to operate the service.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers necessary to run authentication, payments, storage, analytics, or support workflows.

Your choices

You can contact Budy.Study to request updates, deletion, or clarification about your information, subject to legal, security, and billing record requirements.
