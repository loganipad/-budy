About Budy.Study

Built for students who want a tighter test-prep loop, not another bloated study subscription.

Budy.Study is a browser-first SAT and PSAT prep product heading into its April 2026 launch. The platform already combines timed practice tests, a progress dashboard, a guided Study Hub, wrong-answer review flows, flashcards, study guides, and AI Deep Dive explanations so students can move from confusion to the next useful rep without leaving the product.

What already exists in the product

Practice tests that feel like real workStudents can launch timed SAT and PSAT-style sessions quickly instead of getting buried in setup friction.
A Study Hub that starts from mistakesFlagged and wrong answers turn into review tests, topic guides, flashcards, and next-step recommendations.
Progress that does not disappearDashboard views, saved questions, session history, and score patterns keep the student focused on what actually changed.
AI help inside the workflowDeep Dive explanations are built to clarify terms and concepts inside the study flow instead of sending students to a separate app.

Why the product is better positioned

Built around the loop, not just the lessonThe product ties testing, review, study, and re-testing together instead of treating them like disconnected tools.
Made for phones firstThe real competitor is whatever a student can open fastest. Budy.Study is designed to be usable immediately on mobile without requiring a native download.
Readable instead of academic-soundingThe tone, feedback, and AI help are built to feel understandable for high school students, not like recycled tutor copy.
Faster to learn fromStudents should not have to guess what to do after a bad result. The product keeps the next move visible.

How launch is being approached

April 2026 launch windowThe current phase is not idea-stage. It is launch preparation, copy tightening, routing cleanup, and acquisition readiness.
Measured local testingThe go-to-market plan includes local high-school outreach and campaign tracking so the team can learn what actually converts students and families.
Web-first before app-store expansionThe product already behaves like a mobile study tool. Packaging for app stores should come after the learning loop and conversion story are proven.

What Budy.Study should never fake

Strong positioning does not require fake claims. The real flex is that the product already has a tighter learning loop than most generic prep tools. Use the concrete product truth: students can test, inspect misses, study the exact topic, and retest in one system. Do not invent score outcomes or adoption numbers that are not yet verified.
