About Budy.Study

Budy.Study is built to make SAT prep simpler, tighter, and easier to stick with.

Instead of splitting practice, review, and progress tracking across different tools, Budy.Study keeps the full learning loop in one place so students can move directly from a missed question to the next useful rep.

Open Study Hub

What exists in the product today

  • Timed SAT-style practice tests with fast launch.
  • A Study Hub built around flagged and wrong answers.
  • Topic-focused guides and flashcards for targeted review.
  • Progress history so students can track what actually changed.
  • AI Deep Dive support inside the workflow for concept clarification.

What makes the product different

  • It is workflow-first: test, diagnose, study, and retest are connected.
  • It is mobile-usable from the start instead of desktop-only prep software.
  • The product language is designed to be clear for students and families.
  • The next step is always visible so students do not stall after a bad test.

Current focus

Budy.Study is in launch execution mode, not idea mode. The current focus is product polish, reliability, onboarding clarity, and making sure the study loop remains fast and understandable as usage grows.

Product principles

  • Clarity over noise.
  • Evidence over hype.
  • Repeatable study loops over one-off motivation.
  • Measurable progress over vanity metrics.
