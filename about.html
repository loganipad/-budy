About Budy.Study

Built for students who want higher scores without the usual test-prep nonsense.

Budy.Study is designed around one idea: students improve faster when practice looks like the real test, feedback is immediate, and explanations are actually readable. The platform is meant to cut out bloated prep and replace it with clear reps, clean review loops, and visible progress.

What we are trying to fix

A lot of SAT and PSAT prep is either too expensive, too generic, or too confusing. Students end up doing random practice without knowing what changed, why they missed something, or how to improve the next attempt. Budy.Study is meant to make that process tighter and easier to repeat.

How the product is built

Real format firstQuestions and flow are meant to feel like the real test, so practice translates to actual performance.
Feedback without delayStudents should see what happened right away, not wait days to understand a result.
Progress that stays visibleImprovement needs to be tracked over time so students can see patterns instead of guessing.
